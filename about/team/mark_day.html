Mark Day founded Title West in 1987 and as the company's President he continues to oversee its growth and long range planning. In addition to his management duties Mark is the primary closing agent for all commercial transactions. He has closed transactions with values totaling several billions of dollars encompassing every type of commercial property from ski resorts, regional malls, mulit-state shopping centers, industrial parks, hotels, car dealerships and REITS. His clients include national developers, lenders, religious institutions and real estate venture capital groups. Mark is very well respected by his clients as well as his industry peers.

Mark has been actively involved with the Utah Land Title Association having served as its Education Chairman, Legislative Chairman and in 2004-2005 as the association President. In addition he is licensed as an instructor by both the Utah State Division of Real Estate and the Utah State Insurance Department. Mark is also a founder and owner of Western America Title Services, a regional production center providing title search and examination products to agents throughout the state and region.

A graduate of the University of Utah with a BA in Economics, he continues to be actively involved with the university serving on the Board of Directors of the Crimson Club and as a member of the University of Utah Health Sciences Advocates. Mark also serves on the Board of Juan Diego Catholic High School.

Mark and his wife Sandy have one son, Alex who is active in school and sports (a talented lacrosse player Alex has won a Utah State High School lacrosse championship as well as a number of other competition lacrosse titles at national tournaments). They are all avid University of Utah sports fans never missing the home games and traveling to many of the road games also. When he is not a spectator, Mark enjoys the games of golf and bowling. Books are a passion and there aren't many topics that can't be found in his vast library, ranging from Shakespeare and history to mysteries, autobiographies and novels.

President's Message:

Value

We started Title West with the idea that we could do it better. We knew we could bring greater value to the consumer by providing an unrivaled product while maintaining fast and efficient delivery and the highest level of service. We built a title company on a solid foundation of knowledge, experience and energy. We accomplished this by bringing in some of the top people from the escrow and title fields with many years of experience and teaming them with aggressive, fresh, smart young people with new ideas. It is our goal that every customer leaves Title West with the confidence that they have received the finest product and service a title company can offer at a fair and reasonable price. In other words, they receive value.

Integrity and Honesty

We are committed to competing in this industry and growing this company while at the same time always maintaining the highest level of honesty, integrity and ethics. We know these are values you naturally expect from a title company, and no one has higher expectations and standards than those we set for ourselves. There may be less expensive and easier ways to run a title company than the way we do it, but this would involve cutting corners and compromising quality. We have invested untold dollars in automation, training and facilities in an effort to make this company unlike any other. We are title and escrow professionals and not just a creation of slick marketing.

Innovation

The role of the title company is to provide a safe, effective and efficient means to facilitate the transfer of title to real property and the securing of the loans which fund this process. In today's world of technological marvels and advancements, the old methods are just not good enough anymore. Title West is a recognized leader in the industry in the areas of land title records automation and electronic commerce. We have invested hundreds of thousands of dollars in computer hardware and software in an effort to provide our customers with all of the advantages and efficiencies available thorough this technology.

Personal Satisfaction

It has always been our overriding goal to establish long term relationships with everyone we deal with. We do this by letting everyone know that we strive to be the best at what we love to do and we have fun doing it. When you have this kind of positive attitude you provide the kind of quality and service the customer deserves.
