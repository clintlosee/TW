Reputation

Title West was founded in 1987 and is one of the best known title insurance companies in the intermountain region. Our areas of expertise include commercial and residential title insurance as well as 1031 tax deferred exchanges. Over time, our reputation and client base have grown with the dynamic intermountain area. We offer you the resources and capabilities of a large and diverse title insurance company; yet one not too large to give the personalized service you deserve.

Experience

We understand the need for experience and knowledge required in our field by developers, real estate brokers and lenders. That is why Title West is frequently chosen by many experts working in the real estate industry. Through personal commitment and dedication along with ongoing professional training, we have become the recognized experts in title insurance, escrow closings and 1031 tax deferred exchanges.

Commitment

Title West is an agent for Transnation Title Insurance Company; Commonwealth Land Title Insurance Company; Chicago Title Insurance Company; Stewart Title Guaranty Company and First American Title Insurance Company. These underwriters represent five of the top title insurance companies in the country.

Service

Title West has made an extensive investment in automation (computerized title plant, electronic data, etc.) and that has earned Title West the reputation as the progressive market leader. We also offer an exceptionally professional staff. Title West has used hard work, knowledge and a commitment to quality to build a company unlike any other.

We take pride in the quality service we render to our clients and the community. We are confident you will find the staff at Title West to be the most knowledgeable and responsive. So no matter what type of development, large or small, when financial strength, professionalism, and getting the job done right is important, you can count on Title West.
