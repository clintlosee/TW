As a forerunner in the commercial market, Title West has gained the respect of the commercial real estate industry with extensive knowledge and experience.

Combining superior technology with this unparalleled knowledge we provide value as an industry leader in every aspect of title insurance. We have the resources, capabilities and experience of a large and diverse title company while retaining the ability to provide the personalized VIP service our commercial customers deserve.

Affiliated Underwriters

As evidence of our strength and integrity we are underwritten by three of the world's largest title insurers: Stewart Title Guaranty Company, Westcor Title Insurance and First American Title Insurance Company. As a result, Title West has tremendous financial strength backing its policies of title insurance. We pride ourselves in resolving difficult title issues that sometimes arise when special endorsements to the policy are requested.

Superior Technology

Title West has made extensive investments in automation. We have earned the reputation as the progressive market leader in the areas of title plant automation, electronic commerce and Internet access. No one needs to be told how fast technology is changing and the effects this has on all the aspects of our lives and business. This is especially true in an industry so dependent on the rapid delivery of land record information, financial information and communications between far ranging parties to complete smooth and effective closings.

This is why Title West has invested hundreds of thousands of dollars in creating the most state of the art computer network and title plant automation in this market. Our use of the latest communication and e-mail system together with our high speed frame relay (T1) lines linking all our offices enables us to deliver information virtually instantly.

Service

Title West is a company with the strength, knowledge and dedication to efficiently facilitate all commercial transactions. It is Title West's job to close the transaction without hassle. We see our job as this: using our knowledge and experience to find innovative ways to complete transactions others might give up on. With leading edge technology and fully automated services, you can sit back, relax and let Title West take care of the work for you.
